Welcome To Pimpri

Pimpri-Chinchwad, often referred to as PCMC, is a twin city located near Pune, Maharashtra, India, and is known as a major industrial hub. It's a rapidly developing area with a significant concentration of industries, including automotive, IT, and manufacturing. The city is governed by the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC).
